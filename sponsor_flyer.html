About the Workshop

As AI systems move beyond passive perception toward real-world interaction, embodied agents must not only perceive and act, but also communicate naturally with humans.

In physical environments, ambiguous or underspecified instructions can lead to inefficient or even unsafe behaviors. To bridge the gap between human intent and physical execution, proactive dialog becomes essential.

EAD focuses on embodied AI systems capable of reasoning, asking questions, resolving uncertainty, and collaborating safely through multi-turn interaction.
